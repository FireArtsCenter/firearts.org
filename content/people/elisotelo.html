About Eli

Eli Sotelo's furniture and lighting works display a blend of technology and the organic. At the Fire Arts Center of Chicago the sky is the limit as to where you can take your furniture works. As Eli displays the piece can be functional and decorative. Sign up for classes today and begin your exploration into the functional and decorative as well.
