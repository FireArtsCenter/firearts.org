Stop in and check out some of the great student works being created at the Fire Arts Center of Chicago. We are currently adding images to this section of our gallery page so be sure to watch for examples of what you can make as a fire arts student.

Want to see your sculpture displayed here? Sign up for a class and start creating. If you are a past student of the Fire Arts Center of Chicago and would like to have an image of your work posted here, contact us. We will need a clear digital image of your work(s) to post on our site, so keep that in mind before you contact us.
